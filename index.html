🌱 The Bioscience Problem

In modern Malaysian plantation management, optimizing the main nursery timeline of Elaeis guineensis (Oil Palm) seedlings directly correlates with long-term crude palm oil yield stability. This calculator models the vegetative growth of palm fronds over a standard 12-month cycle.

Empirical Reference Models: Baseline trajectories mapped from structural field templates found in the MPOB (Malaysian Palm Oil Board) Nursery Guidelines and physiological curves on Planter DB Malaysia.

🧮 The Derivative Formula

Absolute Height Curve
$$f(x) = 0.05x^3 - 0.4x^2 + 5x + 15$$

Computes the standard vegetative height ($f(x)$ in **cm**) based on baseline age ($x$ in **months**).

Instantaneous Growth Velocity
$$f'(x) = 0.15x^2 - 0.8x + 5$$

Calculates the instantaneous growth rate ($f'(x)$ in **cm/month**). This derivative represents the vigor of the plant at that specific moment.
